1The three rules

Base

Per slide
+$20/slide for 11–20, then +$30/slide for 21–40.
Rush (24h)
Expedited = Standard × 1.5, rounded up to $X99.

2Standard (72-hour) price

standard(n) =
  599  // base, first 10 slides
  + 20 × (slides 11–20)
  + 30 × (slides 21–40)
Marginal rate steps up ($20 → $30), but the per-slide total falls as decks grow ($60/slide at 10 slides → $35/slide at 40). Reads as a volume discount to the buyer.

3Expedited (24-hour) price

expedited(n) =
  charmUp( standard(n) × 1.5 )

charmUp(x) = smallest
  price ending in 99 ≥ x
Flat 50% rush premium. The ×1.5 result is rounded up to the next charm price ($X99) so it stays consistent with the Standard column.
